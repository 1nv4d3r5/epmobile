EP Mobile provides cardiology tools for cardiac electrophysiologists, cardiologists, internists, emergency room physicians, medical students, nurses, technicians,  and other health care workers who deal with cardiac arrhythmias. This is a work in progress. New modules will be added every few weeks, so be sure and keep the program updated.  EP Mobile -- The Swiss Army Knife of Electrophysiology!

Current features: 
- NEW! CMS (Medicare) ICD guidelines calculator
- NEW! Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy risk score calculator
- Dofetilide (Tikosyn) dose calculator
- QTc calculator
- Choose QTc formula (Bazett, Fridericia, Sagie or Hodges)
- Cycle length to heart rate calculator
- Atrial fibrillation risk scores (CHADS, CHADS-VASc, HAS-BLED)
- Dabigatran (Pradaxa) dose calculator
- Warfarin (Coumadin) weekly dose calculator
- Wide complex tachycardia algorithms
- Normal EP values
- ARVC/D diagnosis
- Short QT syndrome diagnosis
- Rivaroxaban dose calculator

Categories: Medicine, Cardiology, Electrophysiology, Arrhythmia.
