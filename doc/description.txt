EP Mobile provides cardiology tools for cardiac electrophysiologists,
cardiologists and other health care workers who deal with cardiac arrhythmias.
In addition to commonly used EP calculations, future editions of this program
will include electrophysiology tips, techniques, and algorithms.

Current features: 
- Dofetilide (Tikosyn) dose calculator
- QTc calculator
- Choose QTc formula (Bazett, Fridericia, or Sagie)
- Cycle length to heart rate calculator

Categories: Medicine, Cardiology, Electrophysiology, Arrhythmia.

EP Mobile is free, open source, and available under the 
GNU GPL v3 license.  Source code is at https://www.github.com/mannd/epmobile.
